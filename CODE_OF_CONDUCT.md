# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, professional, and accountable.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and deliberate disruption
are not acceptable. Discuss ideas rather than people.

You are responsible for everything you submit, including AI-assisted work. Do
not submit unreviewed, misleading, low-effort, or mass-generated issues or pull
requests.

Maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation when necessary
to keep the project constructive.
